Study Reveals Peak Social Media Engagement Times in 2024

A recent study by Social Trends Analytics has identified the optimal times for content creators to post on major social media platforms in 2024. The data, gathered from over 2 million posts across Instagram, TikTok, and Twitter, indicates that weekday mornings between 8 and 10 AM consistently yield the highest engagement rates. The report suggests that creators who schedule posts during these windows see up to 40% more likes, shares, and comments compared to those who post at random. Researchers advise content creators to use platform analytics to fine-tune their posting times for maximum visibility and reach.
